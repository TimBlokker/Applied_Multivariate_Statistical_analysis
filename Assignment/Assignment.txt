Every individual student searches an interesting problem and corresponding dataset
Look in on-line data bases, journal articles, research reports, R, SAS, internet
A report of maximum 5 pages is written and submitted electronically together with the dataset to eddie.schrevens@biw.kuleuven.be 
The report consists of
A summary problem description (<1/2 page)
A description of the dataset (<1/2 page)
The proposed multivariate analysis (no theoretical developments) (<1/2 page)
Motivation of the choice of methodology (<1/2 page)
An R-program that solves the problem
The R-output giving the solution
A summary interpretation (<1/2 page)
Eventually, some  critical notes
The deadline for report submission is the starting date of the first examination period (Janua
